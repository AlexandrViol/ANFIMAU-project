How it all started

The CEO of Anfimau Industry Solutions GmbH is Mikhail Anfimau, a specialist with profound expertise in IT. With over 20 years of experience under his belt, Mikhail has completed a variety of projects, all of which proved to be highly effective and future-proof. As time passed, Mikhail was testing and improving his skills and knowledge in numerous technologies, and domains, as well as overcoming challenges.

At the end of 2019, Mikhail started his solo consulting and engineering career. It was a success, and feeling a lack of labor for the number of projects he was offered and aimed to complete, Mikhail decided to scale out his services. That’s how in 2021, a number of engineers were hired to perform specific tasks and our company was founded.

Quite soon, it has grown into a big team of 30 professionals in several countries in the EU and outside the EU: Poland, Ukraine, Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia, Georgia, and Uzbekistan. Meanwhile, the headquarters together with the key customer-facing roles like Architects, Project Managers, and Business Analysts is located in Frankfurt am Main (Eschborn). We have worked out an efficient collaboration approach that enables our team to stay flexible and converge efforts from numerous locations into outstanding solutions.

We strive to be at the forefront of technological advancements and industry trends. We embrace emerging technologies, best practices, and the latest development methodologies to ensure that our solutions are of the highest quality and deliver a competitive edge to our clients.

We believe that successful software development is a collaborative effort based on trust, reliability, and mutual success. Thus, we foster a culture of open communication, transparency, and active collaboration with our clients.

Ethical & responsible practices

Clarity & openness

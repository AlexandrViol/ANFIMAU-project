What is cloud service and its types?

The term "cloud services" refers to a range of IT applications and resources hosted by third-party providers and delivered on-demand to organizations and individual customers over the Internet. They enable the client devices to access data and cloud applications from remote physical servers, databases, and computers

There are four main types of cloud computing: private clouds (solely dedicated to a single end-user or group), public clouds (not owned by the end-user), and hybrid clouds (mixed computing made up of on-premises infrastructure, private and public clouds)

There are also three main types of cloud computing services: Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS), Platforms-as-a-Service (PaaS), and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS).

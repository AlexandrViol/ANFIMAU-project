What is custom software with examples?

Custom software refers to applications developed specifically for a particular company, tailored to their unique requirements and workflows. Unlike off-the-shelf solutions, custom software is designed from scratch, addressing specific goals. Examples include bespoke customer relationship management (CRM) systems, inventory management solutions, or data analytics tools.

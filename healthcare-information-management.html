Healthcare information management system to streamline clinical workflows

Integrated all business data in one ecosystem and facilitated employees’ and patient’s access to it.

The client

The client specializes in providing healthcare services with a key focus on ophthalmology. The company has already implemented AI solutions for enhanced patient care and illness treatment and is looking for other ways to improve its performance with automation.

Industry insights

  • 1.

    The healthcare sector has always stood at the fore with its undeniable significance for society. This necessitates greater automation of healthcare companies’ workflows to speed up and advance patient treatment.

  • 2.

    With a huge number of employees working under one umbrella – from nurses to administration – it becomes crucial to build effective communication in the workplace. Otherwise, it lengthens patient treatment and leads to a lack of interoperability. This negatively affects decision-making, the quality of patient care, and a company’s profits.

  • 3.

    What we see today is that medical information is still exchanged by fax or through patient records. Information is scattered across different systems, making it difficult for providers to access and share comprehensive patient records. Such informational chaos leads to redundant, disorganized, and inaccessible medical information, which is detrimental to patients.

Business challenge

Enhanced interoperability for improved patient care and data access

The client wanted to integrate a single data ecosystem to boost productivity across the board and improve patient care. Our team was challenged with building a data management system in which data is easily accessed, used and shared.

The solution must give an individual access to every company staff depending on their position and allow for quick information exchange in any form – messages, comments, pictures and video. In addition, the solution was supposed to integrate all the company’s devices to enter the data from them into one data infrastructure.

Our solution

Information management system to access and exchange medical data

The healthcare data system is intended to centralize all the data coming from different sources. Operating on the Microsoft Azure Cloud, it provides a highly scalable and secure infrastructure that meets the stringent requirements of the healthcare industry. Additionally, our team utilized a wide range of both commonly used and healthcare-specific services – Azure API for FHIR and Azure Security and Compliance Blueprint for Healthcare – to ensure seamless integration, interoperability, and adherence to industry regulations.

Striving to help the client deliver best practices for data security and privacy, we made sure the system is rock-secure and future-proof. The solution was built to:

  • Integrate the data from a company’s all devices (photos, videos, patient data) and process it
  • Access the patient data, their treatment records and prescribed medicine
  • Streamline clinical documentation and appointment scheduling
  • Stream live videos for educational, professional and other purposes
  • Allow doctors to quickly seek advice from each other if needed
  • Give recommendations on the necessary medicaments for a patient
  • Reduce the time for doctor consultations

Overall, the solution catalyzes interoperability within a company to enable a superior patient care level.

About us

We provide comprehensive services to help businesses thrive in the digital age. With a fully distributed remote team of over 30 highly qualified engineers, we collaborate with organizations to drive strategic growth through innovative solutions.

Our HQ is located in Frankfurt am Main (Eschborn), where primarily our customer-facing roles like Architects, Project Managers, and Business Analysts are located. Our multinational software development team is distributed across several locations in Europe, having top talent onboard.

Led by CEO and founder Mikhail Anfimau, boasting over 20 years of experience in IT, we embrace design thinking and human-centric innovation to deliver exceptional solutions tailored to your needs. Join us on a journey of cutting-edge technology and transformative success.

History

Mikhail Anfimau, CEO and Founder

20 years of experience in IT

From 2002 to 2012, Mikhail worked for various software vendors as part of outstaffed teams

In 2013, after a brief career pause to complete his Master’s studies in Norway in the field of Industrial Logistics, he moved to Germany to lead a significant project for Philip Morris International. This project involved a team of up to 40 developers and QA engineers. Widely adopted and used by thousands of employees in regional offices across 80 countries, the project was a great success. After reaching high maturity, the project was handed over to a third-party company.

In 2019, he served almost a year as a Senior Infrastructure Architect at Deutsche Lufthansa AG in the central IT department, facilitating the work of various departments and vendors of Lufthansa by providing them with the necessary central infrastructure.

At the end of 2019, Mikhail began his solo consulting and engineering career. He successfully executed several projects for clients such as Vattenfall Energy Trading, Carl Zeiss Digital Innovation, and GEA. The need to scale up services arose, leading to the assignment of more engineers for specific tasks. Consequently, our company was founded in 2021. It rapidly grew into a talented team of 30 engineers, including both contractors and employees, in several countries in and outside the EU, such as Poland, Greece, Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia, Ukraine, Georgia, and Uzbekistan.

We work together seamlessly despite the distance and time zones, delivering our best services and knowledge to our clients.

About us

We provide comprehensive services to help businesses thrive in the digital age. With a fully distributed remote team of over 30 highly qualified engineers, we collaborate with organizations to drive strategic growth through innovative solutions.

Our HQ is located in Frankfurt am Main (Eschborn), where primarily our customer-facing roles like Architects, Project Managers, and Business Analysts are located. Our multinational software development team is distributed across several locations in Europe, having top talent onboard.

Led by CEO and founder Mikhail Anfimau, boasting over 20 years of experience in IT, we embrace design thinking and human-centric innovation to deliver exceptional solutions tailored to your needs. Join us on a journey of cutting-edge technology and transformative success.
